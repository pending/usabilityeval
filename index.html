Usability Evaluation of University of Leicester's website: http://www.le.ac.uk

Hi, we are Team 2 from COGS187A. Our team consists of 3 members: Jane Zhao, Kevin Pan, and May Ng.

We evaluated the usability of the the University of Leiceister's website using Neilsen's 10 heuristics. First, we went over all the heuristics together as a group and discussed the meaning of each, and what we meant it covered. Then, we split to work on the heuristics individually and met again as a group to re-evaluate the website as a group.

To read about our usability evaluation, use the sidebar to the left to navigate to each of the heuristics listed. There you should find a basic description of the heuristic and our evalution, with examples and screenshots.
