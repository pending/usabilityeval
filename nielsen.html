Nielsen's Heuristics

Jakob Nielsen is a usability analyst who put together a list of ten general principles for interaction design, which he called "heuristics". These principles should be used when designing a website to help guide good design and good user experience.

Overview

We are analyzing the University of Leicester's website using Nielsen's Heuristics.

  1. Visibility of System Status
  2. Match Between System and the Real World
  3. User Control and Freedom
  4. Error Prevention
  5. Consistency and Standards
  6. Recognition rather than Recall
  7. Efficiency of Use and Flexibility
  8. Aesthetics and Minimalist Design
  9. Help Users Recognize, Diagnose, and Recover from Errors
  10. Documentation and Help
