Recognition Rather than Recall

Minimize the user's memory load by making objects, actions, and options visible. The user should not have to remember information from one part of the dialogue to another. Instructions for use of the system should be visible or easily retrievable whenever appropriate.

The system does a good job at providing users security and stability by adorning its repositories with a familiar university logo. Visitors increase their sense of familiarity with the different elements in the system when greeted by a familiar face. A miniature navigation tool bar that follows the university logo on many pages of the website also provides an increased degree of efficiency allowing users to return and proceed forward through different parts of the site. Unfortunately, the home page does not have the same layout/design as the rest of the site, which throws off this sense of familiarity.
